The Humble Plumbus

Fig 1: A Plumbus as seen from the front and back

Ingredients:

  • 1 x Dinglebop
  • 1 x Schleem bunch (used is fine)
  • 1 x Grumbo
  • 1 x Fleeb (fresher the better)

Equipment:

  • A long rod of some kind
  • At least one blaff
  • A chisel may make the grumbo removal process easier
  • While not technically equipment, a Shlami will need to be present

Steps:

  1. Smooth out your Dinglebop on a flat surface
  2. While smoothing the Dinglebop, be sure to sprinkle it with Schleem so that it is pressed into the surface
  3. Remove the Schleem from the now smoothed Dinglebop
  4. Using your rod, push the Dinglebop through the Grumbo
  5. On the other side of the Grumbo, rub the Fleeb against the protruding Dinglebop.
    Make sure the Fleeb juices coat the Dinglebop
  6. Have the Shlami rub then spit on the Dinglebop
  7. Cut the Fleeb and use the same knife to remove the Hizzards from the front side of the Dinglebop
  8. Rub your Blaff against the resulting Chumbles until they fall away
  9. Use your chisel to carve away the Plubus and remaining Grumbo
  10. Enjoy your fresh new Plumbus!
